Kevin's House Project A house I made in Computer Science

In this project, I made a house using a program called Tinkercad. I used the 3D modeling software to create a custom house with furniture and outside details. There weren't any specific skills I used for this, maybe positioning and alignment skills, but aside from that I just played with the shapes until I made something I was proud of. The most challenging part was scaling the model to the right size and adding outside models. Below you'll be able to see a picture of the house I made.
